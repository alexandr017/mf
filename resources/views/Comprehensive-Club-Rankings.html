Ranking System Explanation

The Meme Football League uses a standard points system to determine team rankings:

Win

3 points

Draw

1 point

Loss

0 points

Teams are ranked by total points. If two or more teams are tied on points, the following tiebreakers are applied in order: 1) Goal difference, 2) Goals scored, 3) Head-to-head results, 4) Fair play points, 5) Drawing of lots.
